What is World ID verification?
World ID is a privacy-preserving identity protocol that verifies you're a unique human without revealing your personal information. It helps prevent bot abuse and ensures fair play.
How do I verify my identity?
Tap the "Verify with World ID" button on the start screen. If you're using World App, verification happens automatically. On other devices, you'll see a QR code to scan with World App.
Is my data safe?
Yes! World ID uses zero-knowledge proofs, meaning we never see your personal data. We only receive a cryptographic proof that you're a verified human.
Can I play without verification?
Yes, you can play the game without verification. However, verified users may be eligible for special rewards and features in future updates.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us with details about what happened, your device type, and any error messages you saw. Screenshots are helpful!
